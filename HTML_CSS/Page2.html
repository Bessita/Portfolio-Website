Fist attempt in designing a layout of a store in Malta (Valletta) called 'Cekcik'. This was made on Illustrator. I mainly used the shape tool, type tool, colors, opacity, the eye dropper tool, shading and highlighting. To outline the store I used the pen tool.

This is a characteristic style of the second half of the 60s. Visual art and music covers were hugely known for this type of style. Style was influenced from drug usage, so I wanted to depict a woman crying from overdosing and ended up being stuck in her own thoughts and hallucinations.

This was used for an assignment in my course, for game character design. Here I created a steampunk themed game character, which is a half cate and a half octopus creature. The main concept was to combine two creatures into one, that are completly different from each other in terms of species and make it look as natural as possible.

This was used for a school assignment to show the principle of alignment.
